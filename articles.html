Bihar Building Byelaws - Rise or Fall of Bihar

Bihar, once a glorious empire, now struggles with outdated policies hindering its growth. The Bihar Building Byelaws, instead of fostering development, have created legal and economic chaos. This research by DeoYogi Consultants exposes systemic issues—flawed policies, corruption, and mass violations—stifling Bihar's infrastructure progress. The pandemic led to a reverse migration, sparking a newfound commitment to reform. Experts call for urgent policy amendments, aligning the laws with Bihar's modern needs. Without decisive action, these outdated regulations risk crippling urban development, violating fundamental rights, and deepening socio-economic disparities. Will Bihar rise again, or remain shackled by its past? Read more...
