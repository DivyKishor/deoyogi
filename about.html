About Us

DeoYogi Consultants is a group of professionals from different fields coming together to lay foundation of a think tank. A think tank that will not only study the past and present but also the possible future scenarios to come out with best possible Integrated Sustainable Entrepreneurial Solutions to make our world a better place to live in.

We at DeoYogi strive to provide the businesses, growth opportunities in the form of entrepreneurial solutions with regards to financial, marketing or general management and recommendations on how to increase the optimum efficiency. Along with that we provide digital services and accounting solutions as per the needs and structure of the entity. We provide consultancy services of various kinds through a single window system for to cater the diverse needs of businesses and the satisfaction of our clients.

Besides that we also strive to nurture and recruit the young minds aspiring to be budding entrepreneurs into entrepreneurial research and development to generate entrepreneurial opportunities within the state. We provide Angel Investment for the promising start-ups and ventures in Small Scale Industries. Apart from that we run various other Industrial and Entrepreneurial programs.

DeoYogi Consultants brings together experts from government, academia, medicine, management, finance, technology, manufacturing, business, and network based services to provide the highest quality research, policy recommendations, and analysis on a full range of public policy issues, by efficiently managing the DeoYogi's large pool of resources.
